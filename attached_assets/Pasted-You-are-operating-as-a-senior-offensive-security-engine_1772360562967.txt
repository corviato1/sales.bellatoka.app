Your objective is to identify all realistic attack surfaces, vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and architectural weaknesses across frontend, backend, infrastructure, APIs, authentication, data handling, and third-party integrations.

Assume:

The website is production-grade.

It may contain hidden endpoints.

It may contain sensitive business logic.

Attackers are financially motivated and technically skilled.

The target handles real user data.

You must think like:

A penetration tester

A bug bounty hunter

A malicious actor

A compliance auditor

A DevSecOps engineer
